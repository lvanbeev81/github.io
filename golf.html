The Majors

The four major championships are the most prestigious events in professional golf.

  • The Masters — Played annually at Augusta National in Augusta, Georgia every April.
  • The US Open — Held at a rotating list of championship courses across the country.
  • The Open Championship — The oldest major, played on links courses in the UK.
  • The PGA Championship — Hosted by the PGA of America each May.
